About Us

We are interested in applying computational methods for high-throughput transcriptomics and genomics data of various human diseases with a particular interest in the context of cancer. By combining computational approaches and wet lab experiments, we are pursuing to identify factors and pathways contributing to the initiation and progression of different types of cancer.
